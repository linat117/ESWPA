# Future Enhancements & Strategic Roadmap for ESWPA (v2)

This document outlines a strategic roadmap of potential features to support the growth and mission of the Ethiopian Social Work Professional Association (ESWPA).

---

### **Category 1: Revenue Generation & Strategic Growth**
*Features focused on creating diverse revenue streams, strengthening partnerships, and ensuring long-term financial sustainability.*

| Feature                       | Description & Revenue/Growth Strategy                                                                                                                                                                                                                           | Priority |
| ----------------------------- | --------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------- |
| **Tiered Membership & Corporate Packages** | **Description:** Evolve the single membership into multiple tiers (e.g., Student, Professional, Professional+, Institutional). <br/><br/> **Strategy:** Each tier offers increasing benefits (like access to the Resource Hub, job board priority, event discounts). Institutional/Corporate packages can be sold to hospitals, universities, and NGOs, creating a significant B2B revenue stream. | 🟢 High  |
| **Paid Workshops & Event Ticketing** | **Description:** An integrated system to create, promote, and sell tickets for paid workshops, webinars, and annual conferences. <br/><br/> **Strategy:** Generates direct revenue from event attendance. Offering member-exclusive discounts drives membership growth, as the savings on one or two events could pay for the membership itself. | 🟢 High  |
| **Donation & Fundraising Campaigns** | **Description:** A dedicated page for collecting online donations via local and international payment gateways. Create specific fundraising campaigns (e.g., "Fund a student's membership," "Sponsor a research paper"). <br/><br/> **Strategy:** Taps into public and corporate goodwill, providing a direct and essential revenue stream for a non-profit. Clear campaigns with progress bars increase donor engagement. | 🟢 High  |
| **Professional Job Board & Listings** | **Description:** A dedicated job board where partner organizations pay a fee to list vacancies for social workers. <br/><br/> **Strategy:** Creates a new, consistent revenue stream from employers looking to reach a targeted audience of qualified professionals. Free browsing for members acts as a major membership benefit. | 🟡 Medium|
| **Consultancy Directory & Service Marketplace** | **Description:** A premium, searchable directory where accredited senior members can pay a yearly fee to list their professional consultancy services. <br/><br/> **Strategy:** Generates recurring revenue from high-value members. Positions ESWPA as a central authority connecting organizations with expert social work consultants, enhancing its reputation and influence. | 🟡 Medium|
| **Sponsorship & Advertising Packages** | **Description:** Formalize a sponsorship program with clear packages (e.g., Gold, Silver, Bronze) that offer partners benefits like banner ads on the website, sponsored email newsletters, and logo placement at events. <br/><br/> **Strategy:** Moves from simple partnerships to structured, predictable revenue from organizations wanting to align their brand with ESWPA's mission. | 🔴 Low   |


### **Category 2: Member & Professional Development**
*Features to enhance the skills, knowledge, and career opportunities for members.*

| Feature                       | Description                                                                                                                                                             | Priority |
| ----------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------- |
| **Resource Hub / Digital Library** | A secure, members-only repository for professional documents, research papers, training materials, and ESWPA-developed standards.                                  | 🟢 High  |
| **Job Board**                     | A feature where ESWPA and its partner organizations can post job vacancies for social workers. Members could filter and apply for positions.                             | 🟢 High  |
| **Training & Certification Tracker** | A module for members to track their professional development units (PDUs) or certifications. Admins could manage and verify training programs.                     | 🟡 Medium|
| **Member Directory**              | An opt-in, searchable directory of professional members, allowing them to connect with colleagues based on specialty or location. Privacy settings would be essential. | 🟡 Medium|


### **Category 3: Community Engagement & Crisis Response**
*Tools to foster community and enable rapid response.*

| Feature                       | Description                                                                                                                                                             | Priority |
| ----------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------- |
| **Member Forum/Discussion Board** | A private forum for members to discuss professional topics, ask questions, and share best practices, fostering a strong professional community.                       | 🟡 Medium|
| **Crisis Response Module**      | A dedicated section for publishing alerts, coordinating volunteer efforts, and disseminating critical information to members during national or regional crises.          | 🟡 Medium|


### **Category 4: Administration & Operations**
*Features to improve the efficiency of ESWPA's internal operations.*

| Feature                       | Description                                                                                                                                                             | Priority |
| ----------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------- |
| **Automated Backup System**   | Implement a daily or weekly automated backup script that saves the database and user-uploaded files to a secure off-site location (e.g., cloud storage).                 | 🟢 High  |
| **HR / Volunteer Management** | A simple module to manage internal volunteers. This could include volunteer registration, role assignment, and tracking hours.                                            | 🟡 Medium|
| **Advanced User Role Management** | Create a `user_roles` table and an admin interface to manage permissions, allowing for more granular control than the current superadmin system.                       | 🔴 Low   |
| **Activity Log**              | A system that logs all major actions taken by admins (e.g., "Admin X deleted member Y"). Crucial for security and accountability.                                          | 🔴 Low   |


### **Category 5: Website & Content Management**
*Features to make the public-facing website more dynamic.*

| Feature                       | Description                                                                                                                                                             | Priority |
| ----------------------------- | ----------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------------- | -------- |
| **Full CMS for Static Pages** | Create admin interfaces to edit the content of key public pages like "About Us" and "Contact," including managing the team member bios and photos.                       | 🟢 High  |
| **News/Blog Module**          | A simple blog or news section for ESWPA to publish articles, updates, and success stories to the public, improving SEO and public engagement.                              | 🟡 Medium|
